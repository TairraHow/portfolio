CIP1 Project — Master Data Error Reduction
Continuous Improvement · Process Design · Change Management

Chronic master data errors across sourcing and logistics workflows were compounding into planning inaccuracies and execution failures. Ongoing without a structured solution.

02
DFOA Transportation Zone Simplification (SAP)
SAP Systems · Process Standardization · Financial Impact
$100K+ Loss Prevented
Situation
Canada's DFOA rates were inconsistent — no standard method for classifying customers was causing disputes and over $100K in incorrect payments.
Action
Designed a standardized zoning model using FSA codes, aligned rates across three zones per province, embedded directly into SAP, created governance documentation (QRG), and trained Customer Operations.
Result
Prevented $100K+ in losses. Eliminated rate disputes. Created a repeatable, governed process embedded in SAP. Improved customer experience across Canada.
03
Past-Due Order Backlog Reduction
Crisis Management · Stakeholder Alignment · Operations Recovery
Resolved in 4 Weeks
Situation
An RDC was experiencing a significant backlog of past-due orders — creating service failures, internal and external pressure, and no alignment across teams.
Action
Led daily calls asking WHY for each stuck order. Prioritized by customer impact. Built a centralized tracker for cross-team accountability and trained a new employee to own the process.
Result
Backlog reduced significantly in four working weeks. Service levels improved. Seamless handoff due to documented process.
04
Inventory Variance Resolution (SAP & RDC)
Data Integrity · SAP Correction · Root Cause Analysis
SAP Data Restored
Situation
An RDC was experiencing frequent inventory discrepancies between SAP and physical stock — causing order cancellations, poor customer experiences, and loss of confidence in system data.
Action
Analyzed variance trends prioritizing high-variance SKUs first. Conducted daily cycle counts, corrected SAP records, and implemented proper write-off documentation.
Result
Inventory accuracy restored. Order cancellations reduced. SAP data reliable. Confidence in system data re-established across the site.

06
No Playbook — Transportation Request Intake Process
Process Creation · Intake Design · Ambiguity to Structure
Zero Missed Requests
Situation
Stepped into MDA role with no standard process — transportation zone requests came in through emails inconsistently, got missed regularly, and there was no central way to track or prioritize the work.
Action
Designed and implemented a web form that standardized intake, captured required data upfront, routed all requests to a single queue with ID numbers, and gave stakeholders full visibility from submission to close.
Result
Missed requests eliminated. Workload became transparent. Data-driven insights unlocked. Process became the foundation for the CIP1 improvement project.
07
Customer Claim Governance & Cost Control
Financial Governance · Process Design · Stakeholder Accountability
Overpayments Prevented
Situation
High-value customer claims were managed through Outlook with no standard process — missed deadlines, inconsistent handling, poor visibility, and growing customer dissatisfaction.
Action
Moved the process into Smartsheet with automated deadlines, alerts, and defined ownership across teams. Created clear accountability where none had existed.
Result
No overlooked claims. Turnaround improved. Unnecessary returns reduced. Overpayments prevented. Financial accuracy improved.
