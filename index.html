CIP1 Project — Master Data Error Reduction
Continuous Improvement · Process Design · Change Management
40% Error Reduction
Situation
Chronic master data errors across sourcing and logistics workflows were compounding into planning inaccuracies and execution failures. The problem had been ongoing without a structured solution.
Action

02
DFOA Transportation Zone Simplification (SAP)
SAP Systems · Process Standardization · Financial Impact
$100K+ Loss Prevented
Situation
Canada's DFOA rates were inconsistent due to no standard method for classifying customers — causing disputes, confusion, and over $100K in incorrect payments across the network.
Action
Designed a standardized zoning model using FSA codes, aligned rates and delivery timelines across three zones per province, embedded the framework directly into SAP, created governance documentation (QRG), and trained Customer Operations on execution.
Result
Prevented $100K+ in losses. Eliminated rate disputes. Created a repeatable, governed process embedded in SAP. Improved customer experience across the Canadian network.
03
Past-Due Order Backlog Reduction
Crisis Management · Stakeholder Alignment · Operations Recovery
Resolved in 4 Weeks
Situation
An RDC was experiencing a significant backlog of past-due orders — creating service failures, internal and external pressure, and a lack of alignment across all teams involved. No one was owning the resolution.
Action
Led daily calls asking WHY for each stuck order. Prioritized by customer impact and operational constraints. Built a centralized tracker for cross-team accountability and trained a new employee to own the process going forward.
Result
Backlog reduced significantly in four working weeks. Service levels improved. Seamless handoff due to documented process. Demonstrated ability to stabilize a broken operation quickly and leave it better than found.
04
Inventory Variance Resolution (SAP & RDC)
Data Integrity · SAP Correction · Root Cause Analysis
SAP Data Restored
Situation
An RDC was experiencing frequent inventory discrepancies between SAP and physical stock — causing order cancellations, poor customer experiences, and complete loss of confidence in system data.
Action
Analyzed variance trends prioritizing high-variance SKUs first. Conducted daily cycle counts, corrected SAP records, and implemented proper write-off documentation to restore and maintain data integrity across the site.
Result
Inventory accuracy restored. Order cancellations reduced. SAP data reliable. Confidence in system data re-established. Foundational to ongoing operational performance at the site.

Performance Management · Power BI · Executive Reporting

No Playbook — Transportation Request Intake Process
Process Creation · Intake Design · Ambiguity to Structure
Zero Missed Requests
Situation
Stepped into MDA role with no standard process for transportation zone requests — they came in through emails inconsistently, got missed regularly, and there was no central way to track, prioritize, or analyze the work.
Action
Designed and implemented a web form that standardized intake, captured required data upfront, routed all requests to a single queue with ID numbers, and gave stakeholders full visibility from submission to close.
Result
Missed requests eliminated. Workload became manageable and transparent. Data-driven insights on request trends unlocked. Process became the foundation for the CIP1 improvement project.
07
Customer Claim Governance & Cost Control
Financial Governance · Process Design · Stakeholder Accountability
Overpayments Prevented
Situation
High-value customer overage and shortage claims were managed through Outlook with no standard process — missed deadlines, inconsistent handling, poor visibility, and growing customer dissatisfaction.
Action
Moved the process into Smartsheet with automated deadlines, alerts for submission and resolution, and defined ownership across teams. Created clear accountability where none had existed.
Result
No overlooked claims. Turnaround time improved. Unnecessary returns reduced. Overpayments prevented. Financial accuracy improved. A chaotic email-based process became a governed, accountable system.
